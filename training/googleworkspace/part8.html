8.1 Working Smarter with AI and the Wider Suite

The final layer of Workspace proficiency is working at the next level — using AI assistance (Gemini), the wider suite (Forms and more), and automation to do more, faster. This is the 2026 frontier: Gemini (Google's AI, built into Workspace) can draft documents, summarise long docs and email threads, analyse and help with Sheets, generate Slides, draft and summarise Gmail, and more — a genuine productivity multiplier when used well (and Workspace's deep AI integration is a major selling point). The wider suite adds practical tools: Google Forms (create surveys, quizzes, and data-collection forms whose responses flow straight into Sheets — hugely useful), plus Keep (notes), Sites, and others. And automation can connect Workspace to other apps and automate workflows (via tools like the school's Automation skill covers, or Apps Script for the technically inclined). You don't need to master everything, but understanding these — especially using Gemini effectively and knowing the wider tools — lifts your proficiency from 'doing the work well' to 'doing the work well, faster, with AI and the full suite'. This is increasingly what employers expect and what makes you stand out: someone who leverages modern tools to be more productive.

KEY: use GEMINI (good prompts + verify) + Forms (collect -> analyse in Sheets) + automation to work smarter.

Gemini (Google's AI in Workspace): in Docs ('Help me write' — draft, rewrite, summarise), Sheets (analyse, suggest formulas, organise data), Slides (generate slides/images), and Gmail (draft and summarise emails, summarise threads, suggest replies), via the Gemini side-panel — used well with clear prompts and context and checking output (it can be wrong; the AI Prompt Engineer skill helps). 8.3 The Productive, Modern Workspace User

Bringing in AI, Forms, and automation completes the picture of a productive, modern Workspace user — someone who leverages every advantage to work faster and smarter. Using Gemini/AI well is the standout 2026 skill: an AI-assisted user can draft, analyse, summarise, and create dramatically faster — if they prompt it well and critically check its output (AI is a powerful accelerator but an unreliable authority, so you direct it and verify, never blindly trust). This pairs naturally with broader AI skills (the school's AI Prompt Engineer skill makes you far better at getting value from Gemini). Forms is a quietly powerful, very practical addition: collecting data via a form and having it flow automatically into Sheets for analysis is a complete, useful workflow (surveys, sign-ups, feedback, data gathering → instant analysis) that's genuinely handy and shows you can use the suite's integration. Automation (connecting Workspace to other tools, or Apps Script) reflects the mindset of eliminating repetitive manual work. Together, these embody the meta-principle — work smart, not just hard — with 2026's tools, and they reflect Workspace's strengths (deep AI integration, connected apps, collaboration). The result is someone who produces excellent work efficiently, leverages AI as a responsible force multiplier, uses the full suite, and automates the repetitive — exactly the productive, modern, tool-savvy professional employers want. Demonstrating AI-assisted and integrated work — using Gemini effectively (with verification), a Form feeding into a Sheet for analysis, and awareness of automation — shows you work at the productive, modern level that makes Workspace mastery a real career asset.

CHECKPOINT

Work at the next level: use Gemini to accelerate real tasks (draft a doc, analyse Sheets data, summarise emails, generate slides) — with clear prompts and careful verification — build a Google Form that feeds responses into a Sheet you then analyse, and explore an automation. Leveraging AI (responsibly), the integrated suite (Forms → Sheets), and automation to work smarter is the 2026 skill that makes you a standout, productive, modern Workspace user.
