Frequently Asked Questions

Before you enrol.

Can I switch my career track after registering?

Yes. Within the first 14 days of registration, you may switch to a different track at no extra cost, provided there is space in the new cohort.

Is there a payment plan?

Yes. For the 6-month and 1-year tiers, we offer a two-installment plan. Please contact our admissions team for details.

Do I get a certificate on the 3-month program?

Absolutely. Every tier includes a verified certificate of completion, provided you meet the capstone project requirements.

What if I miss a live session?

All live sessions are recorded and uploaded to your student dashboard within 24 hours. You learn at your own pace while still being part of the community.

What's the difference between the tiered program and the standalone package?

The tiered program includes live mentorship, community access, certificates, and a structured cohort schedule. The standalone package gives you the Career Path Document and all courses for self-directed study.

Are these tracks truly remote-friendly?

Yes. Every track listed was selected because remote work is standard in that industry, and the curriculum includes tools and workflows designed for distributed teams.

How do I register?

Click Register, pick your track and plan, and your track unlocks instantly in your dashboard. Then pay to our GTBank account (Ona Services Limited, 0710642377) and send proof to our WhatsApp line to receive your unique access code.
